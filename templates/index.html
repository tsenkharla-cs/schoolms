Class Log Book

This class log is used to record students’ attendance for each individual learning experience conducted in the domain throughout the day. It helps teachers track participation and maintain an accurate daily record of student engagement in different learning activities.

Study Log Book

This section records student attendance and monitors room usage during four structured study sessions: Morning, Evening, Night, and Sunday Reading. It ensures accurate tracking of students during study hours, promotes discipline, and helps the school maintain an organized and productive learning environment..

Student Diary

This section records students’ behavior and academic progress, including conduct, participation, performance, strengths, and areas for improvement. It helps teachers monitor overall development, provide timely support, and maintain effective communication with parents and school administration.

File Upload

This feature allows secure file upload and management of digital documents by coordinators coordinating activities under the Five Areas of Development. Coordinators can upload their activity reports and related documents in DOC or PDF format. The system ensures organized storage, easy access, and efficient record keeping, enabling smooth compilation and management of reports for effective school administration.
